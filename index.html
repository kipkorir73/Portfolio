About Me

Hello! I'm Collins Kipkorir, a passionate front-end developer from Kenya with a knack for turning ideas into stunning, user-friendly websites. My journey into tech began with a fascination for how things work under the hood, and over time, it evolved into a deep love for creating digital experiences that make an impact.

With a degree in Electronics and Computer Engineering from Kirinyaga University, and hands-on training from Moringa School's Software Engineering Bootcamp, I've developed a solid foundation in both hardware and software. But it's my journey into front-end development that truly excites me—transforming concepts into real, interactive designs using the latest web technologies.

What I find most thrilling about front-end development is the perfect blend of creativity and problem-solving. Whether I'm working with React, HTML, CSS, or Tailwind CSS, I love crafting responsive, beautiful websites that not only look great but work seamlessly across devices. I strive to stay ahead of trends, constantly learning and experimenting with new tools to build cleaner, faster, and more engaging user interfaces.

But it's not just about the code—it's about the people and the projects. I believe in collaboration and the power of a great team to build something truly unique. Outside of coding, I’m an avid learner, always exploring new areas of tech, and I'm eager to work with like-minded individuals on innovative and meaningful projects.

If you're interested in collaborating or just want to chat about the latest in tech, feel free to reach out to me on GitHub or LinkedIn. Let's create something amazing together!
